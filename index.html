What's going on in the crypto world?

Cryptocurrencies are crazy. Like absolutely crazy. This week alone we've seen prices of bitcoin at 5k and now at 8k. Investing in crypto is not for the faint of heart. However, these are all growing pains that come with a new technology. Yes, it is fun to look at crypto currency prices, as many people have been getting really rich off of investing in cryptos. But, the important thing to watch is development updates from various crypto teams, which has not let down its pace.


This week the SEC and CFTC held a meeting in regards to crypto currencies. The CFTC chair, Chris Giancarlo, gave a very optimistic outlook towards crypto currencies, stating that our society should embrace the technology. For the first time ever, Forbes released a list of richest people in crypto. Although in my opinion the list isn't very accurate, topping the list is Binance's (a cryptocurrency exchange) CEO Changpeng Zhao, who interestingly has gone from zero to billionaire status in less than a year.
